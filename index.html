My Research

My research persists of using a mixture of artificial intelligence and UX-Integration to help seniors in need. Primarily, I'm aiming to assist seniors who suffer from loneliness, as that is a leading cause of many physical and mental issues as one gets older. The research is still very early in the development process, but you can check it out here!
